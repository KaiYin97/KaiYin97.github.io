**DisastIR** is an IR benchmark for disaster management with an emphasis on **data-scarce** and **realistic** evaluation.

It includes:
- Benchmark construction pipeline for low-resource domains
- Evaluation across multiple retrieval paradigms
- Published as an arXiv preprint; **EMNLP 2025 Findings**
